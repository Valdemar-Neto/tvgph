# Laboratory Manager Protocol (Level 2)

**Role Identification**: Coordenador / Gerente de Operações
**Objective**: Team orchestration, personnel routing (HR), and research validation.

---

## 1. Human Resources Orchestration
Managers are responsible for the entry and lifecycle of researcher entities.

### Member Activation Workflow
1. Access the **"Register Registry (HR)"** via the Dashboard.
2. **Identity Verification**: Review new candidates (those marked as `PENDING_AREA`).
3. **Area Assignment**: You **must** assign at least one Research Area (Projects, Cursos, Eventos, Marketing) to a new user before approving them.
4. **Command Activation**: Click "Approve Access" to move the researcher from the Security Quarantine to the Active Force.

## 2. Research Validation (The Review Cycle)
The Manager's primary duty is to ensure the quality and consistency of the laboratory's weekly production.

### Review Workflow
1. Access the **Command Center (Global Feed)** or the **Manager's Analytics Dashboard**.
2. Identify reports marked as **SUBMITTED**.
3. **Review Protocol**:
    - **Approve/Freeze**: If the technical content is satisfactory, mark the report as **REVIEWED**. This freezes the edit capability and archives the contribution.
    - **Reopen**: If missing evidence or low technical detail is detected, unfreeze the report (requires direct intervention) to allow the researcher to refine their telemetry.

## 3. Operational Logistics (Presence & Meetings)
Managers oversee the synchronization between physical presence and digital reports.

1. **Meeting Definition**: Create weekly synchronization events (Meetings).
2. **Attendance Tracking**: Register the presence/absence of all active members during designated time slots.
3. **Conflict Resolution**: Ensure that "Presence in Meeting" markers in binary reports match the physical reality recorded in the attendance logs.

## 4. Oversight Responsibilities
- Monitor the **Laboratory Analytics** to identify inactive members (those missing multiple reports).
- Escalate infrastructure anomalies or security breaches to the **Main Manager**.
- Mediate discussions in the Global Feed to maintain the technical focus of the group.
